Braised Beef

Description

A braising cut of meat is pressure cooked in a neutral flavoured liquid. This recipe is good for "meal prep" as it is pretty neutral flavoured and can be used to quickly assemble a number of other dishes.

Let's make a simple braised beef.

Ingredients

  • 3-5 pounds chuck roast
  • Beef flavoured better than bouillion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the roast into 2 inch steaks
  2. Salt the meat
  3. Sear the steaks on all sides
  4. Add water to barely cover steaks
  5. Add bouillion
  6. Pressure cook for 40 minutes
