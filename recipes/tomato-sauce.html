Tomato Sauce

Description

This tomato sauce comes together in 30 minutes. Great option for a quick weeknight meal. Yoinked from Marcella Hazan.

Let's make a simple tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 28-ounce can of whole peeled tomatoes
  • Half a medium onion
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a pan
  2. Bring to a simmer and cook for 30 minutes
